PARC: Contrast

edx

edx.org

The edx home page is an example of use of the contrast design principle, because we can see the contrast between the foreground and background that emphasis or create a focal point for the headlines and has a great readability.

PARC: Alignment

Amazon

amazon.com

This home page use the alignment principle, in the navbar we can notice the horizontal alignment, every items is well placed in the page and also an vertical alignment in the categories items and the products.

White Space and Clean Design

Intel

intel.com

The Intel home page has a beautiful design their use some white space more the middle and bottom of the page, what makes the design clean and polished , their use a good white space between the icons and pictures.
