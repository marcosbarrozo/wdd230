Recife Brazil Temple

The Recife Temple was announced on 13 January 1995 the groundbreaking was on 5 November 1996 and the dedication on 15 December 2000, by Gordon B. Hinckley. The Recife Brazil Temple was the second temple built in Brazil the total floor area is  37,200 square feet, two ordinance rooms, and three sealing rooms.
