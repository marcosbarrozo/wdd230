Deodoro Theater

The Deodoro Theater is a cultural heritage of Alagoas, it is located in the central region of the city, in Praça Marechal Deodoro. The inauguration was on November 21, 1910. The Deodoro Theater has 690 seats and is composed of a neoclassical-style concert hall with an Italian stage, armchair and orchestra pit.

Pajuçara Handicraft Fair

The Pajuçara Handicraft Fair is the perfect place for who want to buy souvenirs and others product.

Paçura beach

The Paçura beach is one of the most beautiful beach in the state of Alagoas. There aren't great waves so is more safe for family, you can also find places to eat and rest.

Floriano Peixoto Palace Museum

The Floriano Peixoto Palace Museum is the former seat of the government of Alagoas.Its collection includes furniture from the 19th and 20th centuries, silverware, decorative objects, as well as paintings by painters from Alagoas,

Marapé Dunes

Dunas de Marapé is a set of dunes that are located on the south coast of Alagoas, the beach is very close to a river "Jequiá", so you can enjoy the waters of the river or the sea.
